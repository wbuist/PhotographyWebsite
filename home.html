Featured Gallery

Lone Cross: This cross stands in an open section of Dartmoor, who lies beneath it is a mystery, but its quiet perpetual contemplation of the land is a reminder to reflect on the beauty of the place in which we rest.

Lighting the land: Walking across the cliffs of South Devon, the clouds cleared a little, and a shaft of sun drifted across the sea, Lighting the land.

Tread Lightly: This image was taken at the Giant's Causeway in Northern Ireland. I visited on a quiet day when few others were braving the wind and rain. The seas were boiling from a storm that had passed through the day before. The waves were soaking the rocks and I had to tread lightly on my way to this photograph.
